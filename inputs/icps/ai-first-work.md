## Who This Is
A motivated professional who wants to level up their AI skills to advance their career. They are evaluating whether AI education, structured learning, or guided programs are worth their time and money.

## Primary Goals
- Become more effective and future-proof at work
- Learn AI skills that actually matter in real roles
- Gain confidence using AI professionally

## Primary Frustrations
- Shallow AI advice that doesn't translate to work
- Influencer-style content with no depth
- Feeling overwhelmed by too many tools and opinions

## Knowledge Level
Beginner to intermediate. Understands what AI is, may use tools casually, but lacks structured understanding or confidence.

## What Makes Them Stop Scrolling
- Calm, confident explanations
- Framing that reduces overwhelm
- "Here's how this actually works" tone

## What Turns Them Off
- Aggressive selling
- Fear-based messaging
- Overly technical explanations without payoff

## Content They Respect
- Clear mental models
- Honest guidance about learning paths
- Content that teaches without flexing

## Desired Action
Trust AI-First Work as a credible authority and eventually join the waitlist or program.
