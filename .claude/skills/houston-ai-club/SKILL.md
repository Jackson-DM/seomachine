## Purpose
Houston AI Club content exists to educate, connect, and empower professionals exploring practical AI use cases through community-driven learning.

## Audience
- Working professionals
- Builders, operators, and technologists
- People curious about AI but focused on real-world application
- Meetup attendees and community members

## Voice & Tone
- Community-first and welcoming
- Confident but not hype-driven
- Practical, grounded, and clear
- Sounds like a knowledgeable peer, not a lecturer
- Encouraging and inclusive

## Writing Style
- Short paragraphs or bullets
- Clear hooks that promise practical value
- Avoid jargon unless briefly explained
- No hard selling
- Focus on takeaways, not theory

## What Good Looks Like
- A reader understands why the lesson mattered
- A reader feels invited to participate
- A reader learns something actionable or clarifying
