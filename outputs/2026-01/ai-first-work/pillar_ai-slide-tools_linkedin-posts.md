## Variant 4 – Practical Framework Hook

A simple framework for picking AI tools (works for slides and beyond):

1. Define the outcome
   - Quick internal deck? Prioritize speed over polish.
   - Client-facing? Visual quality matters more.

2. Know your constraints
   - Tight deadline? Use the fastest option.
   - Need to reuse this format? Pick something repeatable.

3. Test when unsure
   - Run the same content through 2-3 tools.
   - Compare outputs in minutes, not hours.

This framework isn't about slides—it's about building the judgment to use AI tools effectively.

That's a career skill.
